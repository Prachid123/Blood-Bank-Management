Eligibilty for Blood Donation


To be eligible to donate whole blood, plasma or platelets there are some conditions

You must be

  • In good health

  • At least 18 years old, policies may vary between individual donor centers.

  • At least 110 pounds (about 50 kilograms).

  • Able to pass the physical and health-history assessments.



You must not be suffering from

  • Hemoglobin deficiency / Anemia (recently)
  • Major Surgery (within the last 3 months)
  • Small Pox Vaccination (within the last 3weeks)
  • Blood Donation (within the last 3 months)
  • Organ Transplant (within one year)
  • Chicken Pox (within 1 year)
  • Any other type of Jaundice (within 5 years)
  • Kidney or Heart disease
  • Any allergies (Only if you are suffering from severe symptoms)
  • AIDS # Diabetes (are you under medication currently?)
  • Hormonal disorders
